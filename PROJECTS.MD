# Projects Portfolio

## BuzzEx - Marketplace Delivery App

**Objective:** Developed to integrate a delivery function into online marketplaces like Facebook Marketplace and Craigslist, enhancing user experience and sales efficiency.

- **[Demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bAAWD3mCmRQ) | [GitHub](https://github.com/AravindVengarai/buzzEx)**
- **Technology Stack:** React Native, Node.js, Firestore.
- **Impact:** Streamlined the selling process for sellers, improved purchasing experience for buyers, and simplified task acceptance for deliverers.
