# Projects Portfolio

## BuzzEx - Marketplace Delivery App

**Objective:** To resolve delivery arrangement challenges in online marketplaces like Facebook Marketplace and Craigslist by developing a React Native app integrating a delivery function, enhancing user experience and sales efficiency.

- **[Demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bAAWD3mCmRQ) | [GitHub](https://github.com/AravindVengarai/buzzEx)**
- **Technology Stack:** React Native for cross-platform app development, Node.js for the backend, and Firestore for real-time database functionalities.
- **Impact:** The app streamlined the selling process for sellers, provided timely updates and delivery confirmations for buyers, and facilitated delivery task acceptance for deliverers, effectively bridging the delivery service gap in online marketplaces.

## NBA Playoff Predictor

**Overview:** Advanced statistical models and machine learning techniques were employed to forecast NBA playoffs and championship outcomes, using mid-season performance data and a comprehensive dataset of NBA Box Scores.

- **[GitHub](https://kimayamcolaco.github.io/CS7641-Team-25/)**
- **Results:** The GMM model successfully predicted 93.75% of the playoff teams from the 2020-2021 NBA season, and the SVM model achieved an accuracy of approximately 69.76% in predicting outcomes, which increased to 83% with further tuning.
- **Methods:** Utilized Principal Component Analysis and Support Vector Machines for predictive accuracy, employing unsupervised and supervised learning techniques.

## DiffPrep - Evaluating Transferability of Data Preprocessing Pipelines

**Abstract:** This project delved into the 'transferability' of data preprocessing pipelines in machine learning, evaluating their adaptability across varied tasks and proposing a more efficient approach to data preparation.

- **[Paper](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1z8VgEsyKtG1HST7_e-BNn2rQT1QgKE4F/view) | [GitHub](https://github.com/AravindVengarai/DiffPrep)**
- **Findings:** Employed similarity metrics and advanced visualization techniques to uncover the nuances of pipeline similarities and diversities, suggesting optimized pipelines for one task can often be effectively repurposed for another, potentially reducing redundant preprocessing steps.

## Minimum Vertex Cover Problem

**Abstract:** Explored the Minimum Vertex Cover Problem through the development and analysis of four distinct algorithms, assessing their efficiency and solution quality with empirical analysis.

- **[Paper](https://drive.google.com/file/d/11Hp4_ue1MzMnjoaARkMggKBd8X4Of8IX/view) | [GitHub](https://github.com/AravindVengarai/DiffPrep)**
- **Conclusion:** The branch-and-bound algorithm guarantees optimality but is time-intensive, while FastVC offered the best balance of speed and accuracy, excelling in low error rates within a four-minute runtime, highlighting the significant impact of local search algorithms.

## Kerberos POS - ID Verification App for POS Systems

**Challenge:** Developed an innovative ID Verification App designed for POS systems to streamline the verification process for age-restricted products, enhancing transaction efficiency and ensuring regulatory compliance.

- **[Screens](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1kfEaINtPV8QG9mH9IS7UCTEH9CFVIgPl/view) | [GitHub](https://github.com/AravindVengarai/DiffPrep)**
